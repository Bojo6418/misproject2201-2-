My Educational Journey

Discovering my path at the University of Minnesota Duluth

Why UMD?

I chose to attend the University of Minnesota Duluth because, for me, it struck the perfect balance between affordability, a strong business program, and proximity to home. Staying close to Duluth allows me to maintain close connections with family and friends while still experiencing the independence of college life. UMD's business school offers excellent opportunities for hands-on learning, networking, and preparing for a career in business analytics, which made it an ideal fit for my academic and professional goals.

I've lived in the Duluth area my entire life, and while no place is without its challenges, I've genuinely enjoyed growing up here. The region offers a unique blend of activities for every interest: hiking and exploring the natural beauty of the Northwoods, relaxing on Lake Superior's beaches during the brief summer, or taking part in countless winter activities like skiing, snowmobiling, and ice fishing. I appreciate that Duluth allows me to enjoy both the outdoors and the small-town, community-oriented lifestyle, making it a place that feels both engaging and comforting.

My Home: Duluth, Minnesota

The Duluth area has been my home for my entire life, and it's shaped who I am today. From the stunning shores of Lake Superior to the countless outdoor activities available year-round, Duluth offers the perfect balance of natural beauty and community spirit.

Whether it's enjoying the brief but beautiful summer months on the lake, exploring the Northwoods during hunting season, or embracing winter activities like snowboarding and ice fishing, Duluth provides endless opportunities to connect with nature and create lasting memories.
