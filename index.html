Welcome! I'm Alex Murray

Hi there — I'm excited to share my journey with you!

This website offers a closer look into who I am and what drives me — from my favorite hobbies and education to my career goals, resume, and even a fun game my groupmates and I created. I hope you enjoy exploring and getting to know me better!

I'm a dedicated student and aspiring business analytics professional with a strong passion for data-driven decision-making, leadership, and continuous growth. I hold an Associate of Arts degree from Fond du Lac Tribal and Community College and am currently completing my pre-business requirements at the University of Minnesota Duluth.


After that, I plan to earn my bachelor's degree in Consumer Insights and Analytics from UMD as well. Alongside my academic journey, I work as the Assistant Kitchen Manager at Bricks Pub and Grub in Wrenshall, MN — a role that's strengthened my leadership, teamwork, and management skills.

Outside of school and work, I love spending my free time hunting and snowboarding alongside my amazing girlfriend, Maddi. Whether we're tracking game in the woods or carving through fresh snow, these experiences let me unwind, stay active, and enjoy the outdoors. They are a perfect balance of adventure, focus, and shared memories.
